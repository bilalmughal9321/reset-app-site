Reset is a wellness and habit-tracking application available on iOS. The app helps users build lasting daily habits, practise mindfulness through guided meditations, reflect through daily journaling, and access curated wellness e-books.

2.2 Usage & Activity Data

We collect data generated by your use of the app to power core features:

  • Habit creation, completion records, and reminder settings

  • Journal entries and responses to guided questions
  • Meditation sessions selected and listened to
  • E-books accessed and reading history

2.3 What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect device identifiers, crash logs, precise location data, or any analytics data. The app does not include any analytics or crash-reporting SDKs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide all app features (habit tracking, journaling, meditation, e-books)
  • To display your progress, streaks, and active days
  • To send local notifications and habit reminders (on-device only)
  • To respond to your support requests and improve the app based on your feedback
We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your information for targeted advertising or share it with third-party advertisers.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely on Google Firebase servers. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Some data (such as your login status and account information) is also cached locally on your device using secure on-device storage to enable offline access.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password for your account.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data from our systems within a reasonable period, except where we are required to retain it by law.

Journal entries, habit records, and other activity data are stored for the lifetime of your account to preserve your history and progress.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information through the app's profile settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Withdraw Consent: Opt out of non-essential data processing at any time by contacting us

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at laurawinnan91@gmail.com. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

8. Local Notifications

Reset uses on-device local notifications to remind you about your habits and daily goals. These notifications are processed entirely on your device and do not involve sending your data to external servers. You can disable notifications at any time from your device's Settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Reset is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.
